Frequently Asked Questions

1 What types of cleaning services do you offer?
We provide a comprehensive range of cleaning services, including residential and commercial cleaning, carpet cleaning, window washing, and specialized floor cleaning.
2 Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?
Yes, we prioritize the health and safety of your family and pets by using eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning products that are effective yet safe for all environments.
3 How do you ensure quality in your cleaning services?
Our quality assurance process includes thorough training for our staff, regular quality checks, and customer feedback mechanisms to continuously enhance our services.
4 Can I customize my cleaning schedule?
Absolutely! We offer flexible scheduling options that allow you to customize your cleaning frequency and timing to suit your specific needs.
5 What should I do before your team arrives?
We recommend tidying up personal items and securing any valuables. This ensures our team can focus on cleaning efficiently and effectively.
